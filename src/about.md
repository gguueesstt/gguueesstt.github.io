**<span style="color: #999;">Jasmine Butt, AKA guest</span>**  

I am an artist, musician, post graduate researcher and Associate Lecturer at UWE Bristol whose current work predominantly explores optical sound technology. 

My thesis (currently under construction, along with this site!) is named *Entanglements in Optical Sound*.
My practice incorporates both photochemical and digital explorations of optical sound, through design and performance.
