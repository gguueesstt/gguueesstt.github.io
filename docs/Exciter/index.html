EXCITER is an ongoing optical sound experiment, where animations are printed onto 16mm film frame by frame, using the DIY frame printing process. The first animation was made in TouchDesigner, using scans of a book about moirée illusions.

I have screened EXCITER #1 at Kathy Hinde's Drawing Sound event, a series of BEEF screenings at the cube, and at the Klinker Club in Frome. It is an ongoing project, and so the film will continue to be added to over time, hopefully the printing process will become more refined as the project progresses, and the sound space will become more varied, with more resolution for higher frequencies and less 24Hz modulation.
