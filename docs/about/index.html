Jasmine Butt, AKA guest

I am an artist, musician and post graduate researcher whose current work predominantly explores optical sound technology.

I am a PhD student and Associate Lecturer of Music Technology at UWE, Bristol. My thesis (currently under construction, along with this site!) is named Entanglements in Optical Sound. My practice incorporates both photochemical and digital explorations of optical sound, through design and performance.
