Jasmine Butt, AKA guest

I am an artist, musician, post graduate researcher and associate lecturer at UWE Bristol whose current work predominantly explores optical sound technology.

My thesis (currently under construction) is named Entanglements in Optical Sound. My practice incorporates both photochemical and digital explorations of optical sound, through design and performance.
